One of Gameplan’s key achievements in the field of sports in India has been introducing and popularizing the revolutionary 3D (three-dimensional) signage for in-stadia advertising. We have not only executed 3D signs in India but also in Sri Lanka, Pakistan, Bangladesh and UAE at various prestigious International Cricket Council tournaments including the World Cup in India-Sri Lanka-Bangladesh (2011), the T20 World Cup in Bangladesh (2014) and the T20 World Cup in India (2016).

We have also implemented 3D signage in football, hockey and kabaddi during premier events such as the Indian Super League (ISL) in football, the Pro Kabaddi League and Fédération Internationale de Hockey (FIH) tournaments in India.

In the year 2023, we proudly showcased two view carpets for the first time in a cricket game in India, that too in the opening game of the World Cup. This was a revolutionary technology that was developed and patented by Amayse, a company headquartered in Denmark.
