About Gameplan

Gameplan Sports Private Limited was established in 1998 at a time when sports marketing was a nascent industry in India. Over the last 25 years Gameplan has worked with sponsorship, in stadium advertising, player management, franchise leagues including Indian Premier League, Indian Super League and Pro Kabaddi League. Gameplan has been associated with Kolkata Knight Riders since the inception of IPL in 2008. The company has also worked with content management and digital marketing, among other verticals in sports.

In 2012 we diversified in arts and culture based events. The Kolkata Literary Meet is now one of India’s foremost culture events and Gameplan has also worked with cinema (commissioning films for the pioneering Royal Stag Barrel Select Large Short Films) besides curating cultural events in classical music and art. In 2018, Gameplan started and introduced Tata Steel Chess India which has since become India’s foremost chess event and also the first to offer equal prize money in the open and women’s competition.
