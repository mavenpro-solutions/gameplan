TATA STEEL CHESS INDIA

In 2018, the Tata Steel Chess India Rapid & Blitz marked one of Gameplan’s biggest groundbreaking achievements in sports. In its inaugural edition in 2018, TSCI was the first chess international super-tournament ever to be held in India. A total of 11 top-ranked players fought it out with GM Hikaru Nakamura winning the Rapid and GM Viswanathan Anand lifting the Blitz trophy.

Tata Steel Chess India has since become India’s foremost chess event and also the first to offer equal prize money in the open and women’s competition.
